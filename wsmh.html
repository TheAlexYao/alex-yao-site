Water Street Music Hall

AI Workflow Discovery — Prep Questions

How this works: Scroll through the questions below, then open a voice memo on your phone and just talk through them. Don't overthink it — stream of consciousness is perfect. Skip anything that doesn't apply, spend more time on what matters most to you. Send the recording to Alex when you're done.

We'll transcribe and organize everything on our end. No typing required.

1. The Big Picture ~5 min

  • Walk me through a typical week at Water Street. What does your day-to-day look like?
  • How many shows/events do you run per week? Per month?
  • How big is the team? Who does what?
  • What's the split between music shows vs. comedy vs. private events?
  • What's the busiest season vs. slowest? How far ahead are you booking?

2. Marketing & Promotion ~10 min

Current State

  • How do you promote shows today? (Social media, email, flyers, radio, partnerships?)
  • Which platforms are you on? (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X, YouTube?)
  • Who creates the content? How long does it take per show?
  • Do you have an email list? How big? What tool?
  • How do you decide how much marketing push a show gets?

Pain Points

  • What takes the most time in marketing?
  • Are there shows that underperform because they didn't get enough promotion?
  • Do you repurpose content across platforms or create fresh for each?
  • How do you handle show announcements — is there a standard flow or ad hoc?

3. Ticketing & Sales ~5 min

  • You're on Eventbrite — happy with it, or looking to switch?
  • Do you track ticket sales trends? (Which shows sell fast, which need a push?)
  • How do you handle slow-selling shows? Any marketing playbook for that?
  • Do you do any dynamic pricing or early-bird discounts?
  • Walk-up vs. presale ratio — do you know it?
  • How do you handle refunds, transfers, comps?

4. Artist Booking & Scheduling ~5 min

  • How does booking work today? Inbound offers, outbound outreach, agents, or mix?
  • What tools do you use? (Email, spreadsheets, Prism.fm, etc.)
  • How do you evaluate whether to book an act? (Guarantees, door deals, past draw data?)
  • How far out are you booking? What does the pipeline look like?
  • How do you track holds, confirms, advances, settlements?

5. Day-of-Show Operations ~3 min

  • What does the day-of workflow look like? (Load-in, soundcheck, doors, show, settlement)
  • Do you have a standard runsheet or is it in someone's head?
  • How do you communicate with staff day-of? (Group text, Slack, in person?)
  • What goes wrong most often?

6. Customer Communication ~3 min

  • How do people reach you? (Phone, email, social DMs, website form?)
  • What are the most common questions you get?
  • How do you handle private event inquiries?
  • Do you have a FAQ or is it all manual responses?

7. Bar & Food Inventory ~5 min

  • How do you track bar/food inventory today? (POS reports, manual counts, spreadsheets?)
  • What POS system are you running?
  • How do you decide what to order and when? Who handles purchasing?
  • Do you ever run out of something during a show? How often?
  • Do you track waste? (Spoilage, over-pouring, expired food?)
  • Does ordering change based on the type of show?
  • How far in advance do you place orders? Lead time from distributors?

8. Financial & Admin ~2 min

  • How do you track revenue? (QuickBooks, spreadsheets, nothing?)
  • Biggest admin time sinks?
  • Any reporting you wish you had but don't?

9. Tools & Platforms ~2 min

  • Quick rundown of what you're using: ticketing, email, social, booking/calendar, accounting, team communication, website/CMS, POS/bar system, inventory/ordering

10. Meetings & Time Sinks ~2 min

  • What meetings happen regularly? (Staff, booking, marketing review?)
  • How long do they take? What's discussed?
  • What recurring tasks feel like they should be automated but aren't?
  • Where do you spend time that feels like it should be faster?

11. Show Advancing & Artist Relations ~5 min

  • Walk me through what happens after a show is confirmed. How do you advance it?
  • How do you handle technical riders? (Stage plots, sound requirements, backline needs)
  • What about hospitality riders? (Green room, catering, lodging, transportation)
  • How many emails/calls does a typical advance take? Over how many days?
  • Do you coordinate lodging/travel for touring artists? How?
  • Who handles day-of communication with the tour manager?
  • How do you share venue specs with incoming artists? (PDF? Website? Every time from scratch?)

12. Audience Data & Fan Intelligence ~5 min

  • Do you know who your audience is? Demographics, where they come from, repeat attendees?
  • Are you capturing emails beyond Eventbrite purchases?
  • Can you tell which fans like which genres? Do you segment at all?
  • Do you know your repeat attendee rate?
  • Have you ever done targeted outreach like "you saw Artist X, you'll love Artist Y"?

13. Post-Show Settlement & Financials ~3 min

  • How do you settle with artists after a show? (Guarantee, door deal, percentage, hybrid?)
  • Who does the math? What tools?
  • How long does settlement take after the show? Same night? Next day?
  • Have you ever had a dispute over settlement numbers?
  • Do you track revenue-per-head (ticket + bar + merch) per show?

14. Content Capture & Media ~3 min

  • Do you have a photographer/videographer at shows? Paid or volunteer?
  • What happens with show photos after the event? Where do they go?
  • Do artists ever share your venue on their socials? Do you coordinate that?
  • Do you record any shows? Live streams?
  • How do you handle press inquiries?

15. Reputation & Reviews ~2 min

  • Do you monitor Google/Yelp reviews? Who responds?
  • How do you handle negative reviews?
  • Do you actively ask attendees for reviews?

16. Compliance & Licensing ~2 min

  • How do you handle ASCAP/BMI/SESAC music licensing reporting?
  • Do you track setlists?
  • Any compliance headaches? (Liquor license, capacity tracking, noise ordinances?)

17. Private Events & Revenue Diversification ~3 min

  • Do you host private events? (Corporate, weddings, fundraisers, film screenings?)
  • How do inquiries come in? How fast do you respond?
  • What's the conversion rate from inquiry to booking?
  • Do you sell venue merch? Take a cut of artist merch?
  • Any VIP packages or premium experiences?
  • Any daytime revenue? (Rehearsal space, recording, workshops?)

18. Competitive Landscape ~2 min

  • What other venues in Rochester are you competing with for acts?
  • Do you ever lose bookings to other venues? Why?
  • How does weather affect turnout? Do you adjust plans for it?
  • Do local events (Bills games, festivals, conventions) impact your shows?

19. Priorities ~3 min

  • If you could wave a magic wand and automate one thing tomorrow, what would it be?
  • What would save you the most time?
  • What would make you the most money?
  • Have you tried any AI tools already? What worked, what didn't?
  • What's your comfort level with new tools?
